## ðŸ”¬ Current Focus: Medical Imaging & Autoencoders

I am currently working on **Unsupervised Learning** applied to medical data. Specifically, I am developing **Autoencoders (AE)** and **Variational Autoencoders (VAE)** to learn efficient latent representations of **Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)** scans.

### Objectives
- **Dimensionality Reduction:** Compressing high-dimensional MRI data into a compact latent space.
- **Feature Extraction:** capturing essential anatomical features without human supervision.
- **Reconstruction:** Ensuring the decoder can accurately reconstruct images from the latent vector.
